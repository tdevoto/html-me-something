Is a vacation to Cuba for you

Cuba has a lot to offer someone looking for thier next vacation spot. The costs compare favorably to other Caribbean spots I have visited. It has something for everyone from lush resorts to laid back rural towns. The locals are very friendly and to my surprise very curious and outgoing twords Americans. I will warn you that it isnt for everyone they are in a transition time moving twords comercialism so ll the comforts you are used to arent always easily accesible. A pro tip is to make sure you carry some TP with you as all bathrooms dont have it.
